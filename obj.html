OBJECTIVES

Objectives of the Counselling Department:
1. To assist students take steps towards success in life
2. To inspire students to aspire towards happiness
3. To help students build mutual understanding between them and their teachers
4. To encourage students to draw up plans to solve their personal problems
5. To help students know themselves better: interests, abilities, aptitudes, opportunities etc.
6. To urge students plan for their educational and vocational choices
