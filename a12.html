Pornography Addiction


Porn addiction is considered a behavioral addiction that is characterized by an ever-growing compulsion to view pornographic content or material.
In the past, a person suffering from an addiction to pornography would primarily satisfy his or her craving for pornographic content by viewing or storing pornographic videos, magazines, and photos.
Warning signs and symptoms of a porn addiction may include:
Being unable to stop using porn or stop engaging in the behaviors associated with porn, despite repeated attempts to do so.
